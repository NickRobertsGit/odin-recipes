Ballin' On A Budget...

...Instant Ramen + Poached Egg

I'm here to tell you that instant ramen doesn't have to be just a means to an end when your wallet is feeling a bit light. There's a couple ways of really spicing up your bowl of noodles without breaking the bank!

Let's get into it:

  1. Boil up 2 cups of water in a small saucepan.

  2. Then, drop your noodles in for about 2-3 minutes, until they become soft and.. noodle..-like? Then add your spice packet (my go-to is "Creamy Chicken").

  3. With the water still boiling, slowly crack an egg and drop the egg into the boiling water (you're going to cook this for about 2-3 additional minutes).

  4. Immediately cover the saucepan with a lid.

  5. Meanwhile, in your eating bowl prepare by adding a sprinkle of sesame seeds and a splash of teriyaki sauce.

  6. After your egg is done poaching, remove the saucepan from the heat.

  7. Carefully pour your ramen noodles and broth into your eating bowl, add a small amount of butter and half a slice of american cheese ontop of the noodles to melt.

  8. BAM, you've got yourself a DELICIOUS bowl of instant ramen noodles.
