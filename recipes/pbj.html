The Tried And True...

...The Peanut Butter and Jelly

First off, don't discount the peanut butter and jelly as a meal. It's 100% a legit meal! The peanut butter gives you some protein and fat, the jelly gives you some sugar and sweetness, and the bread gives you... uhhhh... fiber?

With that disclaimer out of the way. Let's get into it:

INGREDIENTS
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • 2 Slice of Bread
PREPERATION
  1. First off, you're going to want to grab two slice of bread (white bread specifically, if you're not a psycho), and place them on a plate, or maybe a paper towel, if you're cheap like me.

  2. Then, you're going to spread a good amount of peanut butter (specifically crunchy if you're cool) across one of the slices of bread

  3. Grab the other slice of bread and spread an even amount of jelly across it (I personally use strawberry preserves, but hey, whatever floats your boat!)

  4. Smack those bad boys together and VOILA! You've got yourself an old-fashioned, American PB&J.
